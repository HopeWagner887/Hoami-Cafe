About HOAMI CAFE

HOAMI CAFE was founded by Hope and Naomi in May 2026 a shared vision that blends creativity, calm design, and premium coffee culture. We specialize in handcrafted coffee and matcha-inspired drinks designed to bring peace, focus, and connection into everyday life. Our spaces are built with minimalist aesthetics, warm tones, and a welcoming atmosphere where people can relax, work, or simply enjoy a quiet moment.
